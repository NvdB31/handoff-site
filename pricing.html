FAQs

Everything you need to know about the product and billing. Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Send us a message

Can I trial Handoff before paying?

 

Yes! Our Starter plan allows you to create a single project with up to 5 screens.

How does billing work?

 

Handoff subscriptions auto-renew at the end of each billing period. When you cancel your plan, it'll stay active until the end of the current billing period.

Do you have free plans available?

 

Apart from the 'Starter' plan, we have free plans available for non-profit and education. Contact us for more info.

What happens if I downgrade my plan?

 

You can still view the projects, screens, components, text styles and color swatches above the limit of your plan, but they can't be edited or exported. Rest assured – nothing will ever be deleted from your account without your consent.

What kind of support does Handoff provide?

 

We offer priority support on the Essential and Pro plans. The Starter plan only includes support through the Handoff Discord community.

Can you help me to setup Handoff?

 

We offer personalised onboarding on the Pro plan – this includes a 120 min onboarding call in which we talk about your technology stack and offer advice as to how to best adopt Handoff.
